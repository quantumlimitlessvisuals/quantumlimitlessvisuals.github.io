About Me

My name is Aj Williams and I am a freelance filmmaker based out of Toronto, Canada. I have 5+ years of filmmaking experience from working freelance projects, completing multiple film production courses, working with the Film and Video Arts Society of Alberta (FAVA). This Passion inevitably grew into a small business, enabling me to interact with customers to help bring their creative visions to life.
